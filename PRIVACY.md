# Privacy Policy

Shamela EPUB Exporter processes book content locally in your browser to generate EPUB files. No personal data is sent to any external servers by this extension.

## Data We Access

- Content from `https://shamela.ws/book/*` pages you open.
- Your chosen options (e.g., volume selection, hamesh cleanup, TOC flattening).

## Data We Store

- Job progress state in browser storage to show status in the popup.
- No account data, credentials, or identifiers are stored by the extension.

## Data We Share

- The extension does not transmit your data to third parties.

## Downloads

- EPUB files are generated locally and saved to your Downloads folder using the browser downloads API.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue in the repository.
